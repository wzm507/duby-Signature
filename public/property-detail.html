Discover Terra Gardens

Introducing Terra Gardens, the latest residential revelation by Emaar Properties, situated in the visionary new community of Expo Living. Perfectly bridging the gap between urban innovation and natural serenity, Terra Gardens offers a premium collection of 1, 2, and 3-bedroom apartments and exclusive 3-bedroom townhouses.


Designed for the modern visionary, this development is located just steps from Expo City Dubai, offering a lifestyle defined by sustainability, connectivity, and lush green landscapes.

About Terra Gardens

Terra Gardens is crafted to be a refreshing escape from the city's pace. The architecture features sleek, contemporary lines framed by blooming greenery and sunlit courtyards. Inside, the residences are a testament to Emaar's quality, featuring open-plan layouts, neutral palettes, and floor-to-ceiling windows that offer views of the landscaped podiums and the evolving Expo skyline.


Residents will enjoy access to world-class amenities including a fully-equipped fitness center, swimming pools, children's play areas, and beautifully landscaped gardens. The development's strategic location offers easy access to major highways, public transportation, and key destinations across Dubai.
